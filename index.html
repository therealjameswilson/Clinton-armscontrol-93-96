A Planned Volume Needs A Source-First Frame

Volume Control

The Office of the Historian lists Volume VII as planned. This desk keeps the page honest by separating official status, archival leads, released document context, public statements, and inferred compiler chapters.

Selection Test

Candidate material should document major policy decisions, diplomatic activity, and interagency handling around nuclear testing, treaty diplomacy, export controls, regional proliferation, and chemical or biological weapons.

Source Note Path

Every lead carries a repository trail, collection name or identifier, and a first-pass reason it belongs in the Volume VII universe before any document is promoted for chronological selection.

Boundary Control

Records after 1996 are treated as handoff context for Volume VIII unless they document an earlier 1993-1996 decision, negotiation, or source trail.
