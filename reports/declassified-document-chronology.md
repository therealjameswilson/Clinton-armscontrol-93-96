# Declassified Document Chronology

Checked: May 26, 2026

This is the page's first-pass chronology of dated released or declassified archival leads for *FRUS 1993-2000, Volume VII*. It deliberately excludes public statements, broad finding aids, diary-only references, and undated Scout hits. Those records remain useful, but they should support rather than lead the document chronology.

## Selection Rule

Included records must meet all three tests:

- dated inside the 1993-1996 Volume VII range
- released, declassified, MDR, directive, or Clinton Digital Library/Clinton-Russia archival lead
- not merely a public statement, congressional publication, broad finding aid, or undated file-unit hit

## Use Rule

This chronology should drive the first read-through, but packet and source-path leads still require item-level confirmation before final FRUS-style selection. Public Papers and Presidential Daily Diary entries should be used as date controls and context, not as substitutes for memcons, telcons, directives, action memoranda, cables, or PC/DC records.
