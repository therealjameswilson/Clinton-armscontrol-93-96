## Method

The 2010-0083-F hardcopy scans were downloaded from NARA object storage, OCR text was extracted, and the files were searched for terms tied to Volume VII: Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Yeltsin, Kravchuk, Kuchma, Shushkevich, Nazarbayev, Nunn-Lugar, START, nuclear, North Korea, Kim Young-sam, nonproliferation, CTBT, NPT, CWC, China, missile, Iran, Iraq, and UNSCOM.

The hardcopy scans produced several directly confirmed diary entries. The foreign-leader index filled gaps for dates not present in the 2010 hardcopy samples and is treated as an index, not a substitute for the underlying daily diary, telcon, or memcon record.

## Use Rule

Daily Diary references should be used as chronology and pull-list anchors. They do not establish the substantive content of a call or meeting by themselves. Promote an entry only after locating the corresponding telcon, memcon, PC/DC minutes, NSC meeting notes, or agency file.
